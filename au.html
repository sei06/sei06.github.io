AttentivU

What is AttentivU & My Role

  • AttentivU is a pair of glasses that utilizes EEG and EOG technologies to monitor brain activity and eye movements, enabling real-time assessment of cognitive processes.
  • My main goal was removing wires reliably.
  • An inordinate amount of time was spent fixing existing prototypes rather than building new ones.
  • The issue: wires going from the temple (where the board is) to the rim (where the sensors are) were getting frayed.

Why It Matters

  • Even though most of the hardware was done, I really liked it because it was my first time doing hardware work that wasn’t solely prototyping oriented but something that functions continuously regardless of the end-user’s experience (AttentivU was used by ALS patients and kids so it had to be REALLY reliable).
  • Spent 4 months working on a hinge for smart glasses.
  • Learned a lot about sourcing hardware and specific components and how challenging it is to build usable hardware.
  • Got to spend a lot of time playing with and perfecting the look and feel of the hinges (e.g. choosing magnets for a nice sound, testing connectors with springs strong enough to allow smooth locking without requiring extra user intervention).
