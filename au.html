AttentivU

What is AttentivU & My Role

  • AttentivU is a research project at the MIT Media Lab .
  • AttentivU is a pair of glasses that utilizes EEG and EOG technologies to monitor brain activity and eye movements, enabling real-time assessment of cognitive processes.
  • An inordinate amount of time was spent fixing existing prototypes rather than building new ones.
  • My role was to fix the issue of wires going from the temple (where the board is) to the rim (where the sensors are) were getting frayed.
